Services

Not sure what you need? I have listed legal work by category in case it's helpful. Feel free to contact me for a free consultation so I can help identify what's needed, or ask me about something that isn't listed.

Intellectual Proppery (IP)

  • Trademark Registration
  • Research on IP concerns, for example on social media content
  • Non-Disclosure Agreement
  • Cease & Desist Letter (threatening someone else using your IP)

Other Contracts

  • Membership terms or subscription agreement
  • Model waiver release forms (you need a person's consent to be photographed or videoed)
  • Product or service terms (i.e. terms governing purchase of your product)
  • Drafting a custom agreement of your choice

Employment / Workers

  • Customised Freelance agreement (re-workable for use with different contractors)
  • Employment agreement

Website

  • Terms & Conditions
  • Privacy Policy

Events

  • Licences - food licences, alcohol licences, wedding licences, commercial licence (I can identify what's needed, and guide through the process)
  • Conducting a risk assessment and advising on mandatory risk mitigation and other health & safety considerations (e.g. fire escapes, CCTV, minimising hazards, insurance etc)
  • Disclaimers to your clients to protect your liability

Setting up your company

  • A company house application (e.g. company incorporation, name change etc)
  • GDPR registration and advice on data privacy/handling
  • Advice on insurances required

Situations gone wrong

  • We all have those - and if that's the case, let me know if I can help. It could be debt chasing, or if a party has broken a term of your agreement and you need a threatening letter written.
